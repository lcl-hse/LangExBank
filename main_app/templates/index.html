Welcome to LangExBank


LangExBank is a testing platform that utilises REALEC corpus data.
Currently LangExBank supports two types of educational activities:
  • Corpus-generated quizzes. Each question represents a mistake made by student in a Writing assignment.
  • IELTS-like Reading and Listening training sections.
REALEC Research Group/Learner Corpora Lab, NRU HSE, 2018-2020.
